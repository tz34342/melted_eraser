College Students Do Not Use Umbrellas

Special Thanks to: Lily Talley (material), Justin Wang (photography)

The 8th floor dorm flooded after the heavy rainstorm during my first semester at Berkeley. The school took off the ceiling tile and lights but never fixed the roof, so we decided to put up our own “ceiling and light.” The new ceiling floats below and exposes the hole. Water-filled condoms are used instead of lightbulbs. The two choices act as continued reminders of UC Berkeley’s inaction, and that one more day without ceiling and light is one more day “showered in rain.”

Nearly all material used in the installation are salvaged material. The condoms are expired condoms donated from a dear friend Lily Talley. Salvaged materials are not uniform in nature, and thus the composition was particularly challenging.

The installation was highly effective. The ceiling hole was patched one day after the installation was put up. However whoever fixed the ceiling also threw away the installation without notice.
