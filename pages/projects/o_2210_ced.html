On Perceiving Views

University of California, Berkeley, College of Environmental Design

Special Thanks to: Peter Suen + Isabelle de Metz (instructors)

This set of four drawings is a rudimentary study in representation. First two drawings capture the camera as distinct views, each view a separate conception of the camera. The third drawing is an unfolding of all surfaces of the camera. It is to conceive the camera as a whole. The last drawing conceives the camera through the unfolding of time.
