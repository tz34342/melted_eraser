Silence

University of California, Berkeley

Special Thanks to: Chris Myers + Kuan-Ju Wu (instructors)

Sound is information. It is constant information we receive and output at every waking hour. There is always so much information, yet we can never interpret them completely, rationally, truthfully, purely.

Silence is a sound machine that distills all sound, all information. Each black box makes a thumping beat at different intervals while pumping out the blood-colored water. They symbolize hearts bound by anonymity. In a room of complete silence, of quiet unfamiliarity between strangers, we might know each other better than anyone would have expected.
